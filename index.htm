  1. Self-teaching with goals

    April 29, 2021

    As a self-taught programmer, the biggest tip that I have to others looking to become self-taught programmers is to work on fewer, larger projects, and treat them like real businesses. While experimentation and learning the basics are both helpful, they simply won't provide you with many real-world challenges.
